Eco Coffee — Launch campaign

Objective: Launch a D2C coffee brand with high organic buzz and efficient paid acquisition.

  • Engagement +320%
  • CPC -45%
  • Signups 8,000

Challenge

Eco Coffee needed a strong debut with limited ad budget and low brand recall. The priority was awareness that converted into signups and first purchases.

Strategy

We combined creator seeding with two waves of performance creative: product story + ritual moments. We used short-form UGC and long-form testimonials for retargeting.

Execution

  • Creator seeding with 12 micro-influencers
  • Creative test matrix with 5 hooks and 3 CTAs
  • Retargeting funnel optimized for signups

Results

Campaign delivered engagement uplift of 320%, reduced CPC by 45%, and produced 8k signups in 6 weeks. Top-performing creative increased conversion by 32% vs baseline.
